Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Simple and flexible, this dish takes 20-30 minutes to prepare. Save time by multitasking.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup rice
  • soy sauce
  • salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 Chinese sausages
  • 1 cup corn/peas/other vegetable

Steps

  1. Pour rice in pressure cooker, add an inch of water, and cook.
  2. Mix 3 eggs in a bowl together.
  3. Chop sausages into small slices.
  4. Add a thin layer of oil to a wok and set fire to medium.
  5. Pour eggs in, add salt, slice into pieces when solidified.
  6. Add sausages and vegetable.
  7. Add rice and soy sauce; stir until all rice is slightly brown.
