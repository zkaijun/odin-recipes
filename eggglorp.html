Egg Glorp

Description

Stimulates appendix growth. Best served with roasted cellophane.

Ingredients

  • Himalayan whale blubber
  • distilled brimstone
  • 7 wasp mushrooms
  • scorpion extract
  • aspic
  • bananas

Steps

  1. Dump the brimstone and aspic in a latrine and let fester for 85.87 days.
  2. Ingest and regurgitate mushrooms thrice and stuff in whale blubber.
  3. Cut off the hard, brown banana stem and fry well in palm oil.
  4. Combine all ingredients in cauldron and let simmer for one month under a blue corn moon.
